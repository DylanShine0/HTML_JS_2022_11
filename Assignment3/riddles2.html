Riddles

  1. Question

    I have cities, but no houses. I have mountains, but no trees. I have water, but no fish. What am I?


    Answer

    A Map

  2. Question

    What is stronger than steel but can’t handle the sun?


    Answer

    Ice

  3. Question

    What is it that no one wants, but no one wants to lose?


    Answer

    A lawsuit

  4. Question

    You are my brother, but I am not your brother. Who am I?


    Answer

    I am your sister

  5. Question

    What bird do you associate with lifting weight?


    Answer

    A crane

  6. Question

    I can be long or can be short, I can be black, white, brown, or purple. You can find me the world over and I am often the main feature. What am I?


    Answer

    Rice

  7. Question

    Nobody has ever walked this way. Which way is it?


    Answer

    The Milky Way

  8. Question

    You bury me when I’m alive and dig me up when I’m dead. What am I?


    Answer

    A plant

  9. Question

    You’ve heard me before, yet you hear me again. Then I die until you call me again. What am I?


    Answer

    An echo

  10. Question

    You answer me, but I never ask you a question. What am I?


    Answer

    A telephone
